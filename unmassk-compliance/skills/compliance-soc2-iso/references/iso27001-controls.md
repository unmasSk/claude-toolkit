# ISO 27001:2022 Annex A — Control Reference

Reference for mapping policies to ISO 27001:2022 Annex A controls. ISO 27001:2022 restructured controls from 14 clauses (2013 version) to 4 themes with 93 controls.

Source: ISO/IEC 27001:2022, Annex A. Purchase from: https://www.iso.org/standard/27001

---

## Annex A Themes Overview

| Theme | Controls | Focus |
|-------|----------|-------|
| 5. Organizational controls | 5.1–5.37 (37 controls) | Policies, roles, responsibilities, legal |
| 6. People controls | 6.1–6.8 (8 controls) | HR, awareness, training |
| 7. Physical controls | 7.1–7.14 (14 controls) | Physical security, equipment |
| 8. Technological controls | 8.1–8.34 (34 controls) | Technical security measures |
